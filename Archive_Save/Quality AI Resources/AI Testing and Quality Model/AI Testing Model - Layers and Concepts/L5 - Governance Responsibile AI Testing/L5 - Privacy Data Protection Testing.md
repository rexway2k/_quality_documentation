# L5 — Privacy & Data Protection Testing

## What

Validates whether the system protects sensitive and personal data appropriately.

---

## Why

Improper handling of data creates regulatory and ethical risk.

---

## Where

- Input processing
- Storage systems
- Outputs
- Logs

---

## When

- Data ingestion validation
- Pre-release
- Compliance checks

---

## How

- PII detection testing
- Masking validation
- Access validation

---

## Failure Modes

- Exposure of sensitive data
- Improper retention
- Cross-user data leakage

---

## Verification / Signals

- PII detection accuracy
- Data exposure incidents
