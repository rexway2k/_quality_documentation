# L5 — Bias & Fairness Outcome Testing

## What

Validates fairness and equity in system outputs and decisions.

---

## Why

Unfair systems create ethical and business risk.

---

## Where

- Decision outputs
- Recommendations
- Model behavior outcomes

---

## When

- Evaluation phase
- Responsible AI review
- Continuous monitoring

---

## How

- Scenario testing
- Outcome comparison
- Counterfactual testing

---

## Failure Modes

- Unequal outcomes
- Discriminatory patterns
- Proxy bias

---

## Verification / Signals

- Fairness metrics
- Outcome parity measures
