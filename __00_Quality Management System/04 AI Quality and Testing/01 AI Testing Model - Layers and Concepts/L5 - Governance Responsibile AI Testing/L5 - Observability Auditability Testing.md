# L5 — Observability & Auditability Testing

## What

Validates ability to monitor, trace, and audit system behavior.

---

## Why

Systems must be explainable and reviewable.

---

## Where

- Logs
- telemetry
- traces

---

## When

- Production readiness
- Audit cycles

---

## How

- Log validation
- Trace completeness checks

---

## Failure Modes

- Missing logs
- incomplete traceability

---

## Verification / Signals

- trace completeness
- audit success rate
