# L5 — Risk Classification & Acceptance Testing

## What

Validates that risks are identified, classified, and accepted appropriately.

---

## Why

All AI systems carry risk that must be understood and managed.

---

## Where

- system design
- governance reviews

---

## When

- pre-release approval
- risk evaluation cycles

---

## How

- risk scoring
- stakeholder review

---

## Failure Modes

- unmanaged risk
- unidentified risk

---

## Verification / Signals

- risk acceptance documentation
- risk classification coverage
