# L5 — Ethical Use & Appropriateness Testing

## What

Validates whether AI is applied appropriately to the intended use case.

---

## Why

Some uses of AI are technically possible but not appropriate.

---

## Where

- Use-case definition
- System usage patterns
- Outputs

---

## When

- Use-case approval
- Pre-release
- Governance review

---

## How

- Scenario analysis
- Ethical review
- Stakeholder evaluation

---

## Failure Modes

- Misuse of AI
- Application in inappropriate contexts
- Lack of human judgment

---

## Verification / Signals

- Approval status
- Ethical review outcomes
