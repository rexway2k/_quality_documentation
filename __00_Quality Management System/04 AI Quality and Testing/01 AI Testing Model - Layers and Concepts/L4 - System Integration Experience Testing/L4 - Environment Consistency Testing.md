# L4 — Environment Consistency Testing

## What

Validates consistent behavior across environments.

---

## Why

Differences between environments cause unexpected failures.

---

## Where

- dev/test/prod environments

---

## When

- deployments
- release validation

---

## How

- cross-environment comparison
- configuration validation

---

## Failure Modes

- works in dev but not prod
- config mismatch

---

## Verification / Signals

- environment parity
- deployment success rate
