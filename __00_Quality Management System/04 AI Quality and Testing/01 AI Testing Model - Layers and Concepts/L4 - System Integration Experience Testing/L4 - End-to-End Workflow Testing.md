# L4 — End-to-End Workflow Testing

## What

Validates complete workflows from user input to final system outcome.

---

## Why

Even if parts work, the system may fail across transitions.

---

## Where

- Multi-service flows
- User-to-system interactions

---

## When

- Pre-release
- Regression cycles

---

## How

- Full scenario testing
- Business flow validation
- Trace-based validation

---

## Failure Modes

- Partial completion
- Incorrect flow transitions
- Silent failures

---

## Verification / Signals

- Workflow completion rate
- End-to-end success rate
