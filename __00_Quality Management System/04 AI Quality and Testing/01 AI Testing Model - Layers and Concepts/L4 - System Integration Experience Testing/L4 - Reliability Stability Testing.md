# L4 — Reliability & Stability Testing

Validates system consistency over time.

---

## Why

Unstable systems reduce trust and usability.

---

## Where

- production systems
- long-running processes

---

## When

- release validation
- monitoring

---

## How

- uptime checks
- repeat execution testing

---

## Failure Modes

- intermittent failure
- instability

---

## Verification / Signals

- uptime
- failure frequency
