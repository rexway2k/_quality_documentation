# L0 — Data Completeness Testing

## What
Validates that all required data elements are present.

---

## Why
Incomplete data leads to partial reasoning and incorrect outputs.

---

## Where
- Structured datasets
- Knowledge bases
- Input forms

---

## When
- Data ingestion
- Pre-processing
- Before model consumption

---

## How
- Required field validation
- Missing value analysis

---

## Failure Modes
- Missing required attributes
- Sparse datasets

---

## Verification / Signals
- % completeness
- Missing field rate
