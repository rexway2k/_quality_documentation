# L0 — Data Bias & Representativeness Testing

## What
Evaluates whether data fairly represents the intended population or domain.

---

## Why
Biased data leads to biased outputs and unfair system behavior.

---

## Where
- Training datasets
- Evaluation datasets

---

## When
- Dataset creation
- Model evaluation

---

## How
- Distribution analysis
- Sampling evaluation

---

## Failure Modes
- Underrepresented groups
- Overrepresentation bias

---

## Verification / Signals
- Distribution balance metrics
- Representation coverage
