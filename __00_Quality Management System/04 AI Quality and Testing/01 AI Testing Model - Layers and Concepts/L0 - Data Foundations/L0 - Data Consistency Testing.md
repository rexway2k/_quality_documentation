# L0 — Data Consistency Testing

## What
Ensures data is internally consistent across datasets and fields.

---

## Why
Conflicting data creates ambiguity and unreliable outputs.

---

## Where
- Across tables
- Across documents
- Across systems

---

## When
- Data integration
- Data transformation
- Ongoing validation

---

## How
- Cross-field validation
- Referential integrity checks

---

## Failure Modes
- Conflicting values
- Inconsistent representations

---

## Verification / Signals
- Consistency rule pass rate
- Conflict detection rate
