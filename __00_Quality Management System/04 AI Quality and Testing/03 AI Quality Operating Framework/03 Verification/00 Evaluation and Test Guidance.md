# Evaluation and Test Guidance

## Purpose

This section captures how the framework should be evaluated and tested in practice.

## Core Verification Approach

Verification should answer:
- what is the expected outcome
- who the consumer is
- what could fail
- how correctness will be proven

## Suggested Review Areas

- contract clarity
- ambiguity handling
- risk management
- execution quality
- verification alignment
- output quality

## Suggested Practice

Use evaluation scenarios to check whether the AI-enabled behavior:
- aligns to the defined contract
- surfaces ambiguity instead of hiding it
- produces explainable and traceable output
- proceeds with appropriately bounded uncertainty
