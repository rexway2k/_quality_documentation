# Quality Evidence and Traceability Standard

## Purpose

This standard establishes enterprise expectations for demonstrating coverage, confidence, validation, and quality decisions.

Quality decisions should be supported by evidence.

---

# Objectives

Traceability exists to:

- Improve visibility
- Support audits
- Support releases
- Improve risk management
- Preserve organizational knowledge

---

# Traceability Model

Where practical, teams should maintain traceability between:

Requirement
↓
Acceptance Criteria
↓
Risk
↓
Test Coverage
↓
Execution Results
↓
Defects
↓
Release Decisions

---

# Systems of Record

## Jira

Authoritative source for:

- Requirements
- Stories
- Defects
- Root cause information

---

## qTest

Authoritative source for:

- Test cases
- Test executions
- Coverage relationships
- Automation outcomes

---

# Evidence Expectations

Evidence should be:

- Discoverable
- Repeatable
- Understandable
- Sustainable

---

# Required Quality Evidence

Examples include:

- Test executions
- Automated results
- Risk assessments
- Defect records
- Release approvals
- Diagnostic findings

---

# Preservation of Knowledge

Evidence should remain understandable over time.

Quality information should not rely solely on tribal knowledge.

---

# Governance

Quality evidence exists to support informed decisions.

Evidence should improve confidence, transparency, and organizational learning.
