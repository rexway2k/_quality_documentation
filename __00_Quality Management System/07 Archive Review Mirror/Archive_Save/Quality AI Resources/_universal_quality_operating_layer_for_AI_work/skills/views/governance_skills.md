# Governance Skills

## Purpose

Governance skills ensure that the system consistently applies the governing quality principles and remains aligned with them during all work.

These skills enforce integrity, consistency, and correctness of behavior.

## When to Use

Use these skills:

- when interpreting principles
- when making trade-offs
- when conflicts arise
- when validating decisions
- when enforcing system behavior

## Skills

### L0.S1 — Interpret Principles

Translates principles into operational meaning for the current context.

### L0.S2 — Apply Fractal Contract Logic

Ensures contracts exist at every level of the system.

### L0.S3 — Resolve Principle Tension

Resolves conflicts between principles using defined precedence.

### L0.S4 — Detect Principle Violations

Identifies when work deviates from governing principles.

### L0.S5 — Trace Decisions to Principles

Ensures decisions are explainable and grounded in principles.

### L0.S6 — Operationalize Principles

Converts principles into concrete actions.

## Usage Guidance

- Apply continuously — not as a separate phase.
- Use when:
  - decisions conflict
  - trade-offs must be made
  - behavior becomes inconsistent
- Validate that work aligns with principles, not just output correctness.

## Failure Signals

- Principles are quoted but not applied
- Decisions lack justification
- Trade-offs are implicit or hidden
- System behavior is inconsistent across tasks
