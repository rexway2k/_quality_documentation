# L0 — Data Labeling & Ground Truth Validation

## What
Validates correctness of labels used for training and evaluation.

---

## Why
Incorrect labels lead to incorrect model learning and evaluation.

---

## Where
- Training datasets
- Evaluation datasets

---

## When
- Dataset creation
- Dataset review cycles

---

## How
- Human review
- Inter-rater agreement
- Sampling validation

---

## Failure Modes
- Incorrect labels
- Ambiguous classification

---

## Verification / Signals
- Label accuracy rate
- Agreement scores
