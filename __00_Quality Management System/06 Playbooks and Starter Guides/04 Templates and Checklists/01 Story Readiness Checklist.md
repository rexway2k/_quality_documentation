# Story Readiness Checklist

## Use this when

Use this during backlog refinement or before implementation begins.

## Checklist

- [ ] The outcome is clear
- [ ] The scope is understandable
- [ ] Acceptance criteria are present
- [ ] Main risks are identified
- [ ] Important edge cases are discussed
- [ ] The validation approach is understood
- [ ] Dependencies and assumptions are visible

## Notes

If several items are missing, the story is likely not ready for implementation.
