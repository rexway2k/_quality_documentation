# AI Feature Quality Playbook

## Purpose

This guide helps teams think about quality for AI and LLM-based features in a practical way.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this when building, reviewing, or releasing AI-enabled features.

## Core Principle

AI quality is not a single test. It spans multiple layers and concerns, including model behavior, retrieval quality, workflow behavior, and governance.

## What to Think About

- what the AI feature is expected to do
- what could go wrong
- what risks are most important
- what evidence is needed before release
- what monitoring is needed after deployment

## Practical Areas to Review

- model behavior and output quality
- retrieval and grounding quality
- workflow and agent logic
- safety, privacy, bias, and compliance concerns
- observability and post-release monitoring

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The intended behavior is clear
- [ ] Key risks are known
- [ ] Validation covers the right layer
- [ ] Evaluation and monitoring plans exist
- [ ] Governance concerns are reviewed
