# Production Support and Continuous Improvement Playbook

## Purpose

This guide helps teams use support and production feedback as a quality improvement loop.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this during incident triage, post-release review, and ongoing improvement work.

## Core Idea

Production insight is valuable. It helps teams understand whether the system is behaving as intended and what should be improved to reduce future risk.

## Practical Guidance

- review production issues with context and impact
- capture the learning clearly
- identify whether the issue was a defect, a process gap, an assumption failure, or a validation gap
- turn the learning into action where possible

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The impact is understood
- [ ] The issue is reviewed with context
- [ ] The team captures the learning
- [ ] Improvements or prevention actions are identified
