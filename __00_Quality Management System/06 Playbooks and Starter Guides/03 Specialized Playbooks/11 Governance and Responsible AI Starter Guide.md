# Governance and Responsible AI Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide provides a concise starting point for responsible AI concerns such as privacy, security, bias, compliance, and human oversight.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this when reviewing AI features, planning AI releases, or discussing governance concerns that may affect the business or users.

## Core Idea

Governance is not separate from quality. It is part of ensuring the AI feature is appropriate, safe, and acceptable for use.

## Practical Focus Areas

- privacy and data handling
- security and misuse concerns
- fairness and bias considerations
- compliance and policy alignment
- human oversight and review where needed

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The key governance concerns are identified
- [ ] The appropriate review or approval path is understood
- [ ] Risk is discussed in context
- [ ] Monitoring or follow-up is planned where needed
