# Evidence and Traceability Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps teams capture the right evidence without making the process too heavy.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this when preparing for release, review, audit, or follow-up on quality decisions.

## Core Idea

Evidence should support understanding and decision-making. It should not become paperwork for its own sake.

## What Good Evidence Looks Like

Useful evidence is:
- relevant to the decision being made
- easy to understand
- sufficient to show confidence or concern
- easy to find later if needed

## Simple Guidance

Capture evidence for:
- what changed
- what was validated
- what risks remain
- what decisions were made
- what follow-up is needed

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The evidence connects to a real decision or outcome
- [ ] It is understandable and easy to review
- [ ] It is sufficient for the change or release risk
- [ ] It can be found later if needed
