# Quality Metrics Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps teams use a small set of quality metrics in a practical and meaningful way.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this when discussing quality trends, team health, release confidence, or quality improvement opportunities.

## Core Idea

Metrics should support understanding and improvement. They should not become a bureaucracy or a substitute for judgment.

## Useful Metric Categories

- defect trends and severity
- validation coverage or evidence availability
- release readiness outcomes
- incident and support patterns
- automation and regression protection signals

## Simple Guidance

Choose a small set of metrics that help answer:
- are we improving
- where are we carrying risk
- what needs attention now

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The metric is tied to a real quality concern
- [ ] The team can explain why it matters
- [ ] It supports action rather than reporting for its own sake
- [ ] It is reviewed in context
