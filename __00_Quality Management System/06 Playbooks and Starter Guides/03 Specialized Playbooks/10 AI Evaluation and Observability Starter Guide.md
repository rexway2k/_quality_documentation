# AI Evaluation and Observability Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps teams think about how to evaluate and observe AI-enabled features in a practical way.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this when planning AI validation, reviewing AI releases, or setting up observability for AI behavior.

## Core Idea

AI systems need evaluation and observability because behavior can be probabilistic and context-dependent.

## Practical Focus Areas

- quality of outputs and responses
- retrieval or grounding quality where relevant
- workflow behavior and downstream effects
- monitoring signals and feedback loops

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The team knows what behavior matters most
- [ ] Evaluation methods match the risk
- [ ] Monitoring or observability is planned
- [ ] The team can review results and act on them
