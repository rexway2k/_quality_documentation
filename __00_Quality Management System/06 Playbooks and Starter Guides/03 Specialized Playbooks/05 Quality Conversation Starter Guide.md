# Quality Conversation Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps teams begin useful quality conversations during planning, refinement, review, and release discussions.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this in meetings where the team needs to quickly align on quality concerns, expectations, risks, or readiness.

## Useful Conversation Prompts

- What outcome matters most here?
- What could go wrong?
- Where do we need confidence?
- What evidence is enough for this change?
- What should we monitor after release?
- What are we still uncertain about?

## Simple Meeting Flow

1. Clarify the outcome
2. Surface the primary risks
3. Identify the validation approach
4. Decide what evidence is needed
5. Note any follow-up or support concerns

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The purpose is clear
- [ ] The main risks are discussed
- [ ] The validation approach is understood
- [ ] The team agrees on the evidence needed
- [ ] Any open concerns are captured
