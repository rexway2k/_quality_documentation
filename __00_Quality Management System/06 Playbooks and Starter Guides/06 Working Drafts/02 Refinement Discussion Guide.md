# Refinement Discussion Guide

## Purpose

Use this guide during backlog refinement to keep the conversation focused on the work that matters most.

## Suggested Discussion Flow

1. What outcome matters most?
2. What is in scope and out of scope?
3. What could go wrong?
4. What validation is needed?
5. What needs to be clarified before implementation?

## Quick Tips

- keep the discussion focused on readiness and risk
- capture assumptions and dependencies clearly
- identify the validation approach early
- note any item that needs follow-up before build begins
