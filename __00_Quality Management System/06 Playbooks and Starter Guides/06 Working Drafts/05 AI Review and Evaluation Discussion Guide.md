# AI Review and Evaluation Discussion Guide

## Purpose

Use this guide when reviewing AI-enabled work or discussing readiness for release.

## Suggested Discussion Flow

1. What is the AI feature intended to do?
2. What are the highest-risk behaviors or failure modes?
3. What evaluation evidence is available?
4. What monitoring or observability is needed?
5. What governance or policy concerns need review?

## Quick Tips

- focus on the most important quality and risk concerns
- keep the discussion practical and evidence-based
- connect evaluation to release and support decisions
- note any follow-up work or open concerns
