# Release Readiness Discussion Guide

## Purpose

Use this guide to structure a short release readiness discussion before go-live.

## Suggested Discussion Flow

1. What changed?
2. What outcome matters most?
3. What validation was completed?
4. What risks or known issues remain?
5. Are the support and monitoring plans ready?
6. Is the release recommendation clear?

## Quick Tips

- keep the discussion tied to decision-making
- distinguish what is known from what is still uncertain
- align on the release recommendation clearly
- capture any remaining action items
