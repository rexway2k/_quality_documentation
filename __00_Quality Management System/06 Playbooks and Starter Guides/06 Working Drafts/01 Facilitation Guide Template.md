# Facilitation Guide Template

## Purpose

Use this template to structure lightweight quality-focused meetings or discussion sessions.

## Suggested Agenda

1. Purpose of the meeting
2. What outcome matters most
3. What could go wrong
4. What evidence or validation is needed
5. What action or decision is required

## Facilitation Tips

- keep the discussion focused on the main risk and decision
- avoid turning the conversation into a full standards review
- capture clear next steps and owners
- summarize the decision or recommendation at the end
