# Risk Review Guide

## Purpose

Use this guide for short risk review conversations during planning, delivery, or release preparation.

## Suggested Discussion Flow

1. What outcome matters most?
2. What is the most important failure mode?
3. What is the impact if it fails?
4. What validation or control is needed?
5. What is the decision or follow-up action?

## Quick Tips

- focus on the most important risk rather than every possible risk
- connect risk to the delivery decision
- avoid turning the discussion into a long analysis session
- capture open concerns and next steps
