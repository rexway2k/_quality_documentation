# Quality Participation and Ownership Playbook

## Purpose

This guide helps teams make quality ownership practical and clear.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this when setting expectations for team roles, quality responsibilities, or delivery collaboration.

## Core Idea

Quality is shared, but ownership still needs to be clear. The goal is to avoid confusion about who is responsible for what.

## Practical Guidance

- Product and business roles help shape expectations and priorities
- Engineering roles build, validate, and support change
- QA/QE roles help define and coordinate validation confidence
- Support and operations help with release readiness and post-release learning

## Simple Ownership model

- Responsible: performs the work
- Accountable: ensures the work is completed successfully
- Consulted: provides input or review
- Informed: receives awareness of outcomes or decisions

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] Ownership is visible
- [ ] The team knows who contributes what
- [ ] Quality conversations happen early and often
- [ ] Important stakeholders are consulted when risk is significant
