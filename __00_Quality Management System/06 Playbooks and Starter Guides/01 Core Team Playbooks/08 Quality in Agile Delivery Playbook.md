# Quality in Agile Delivery Playbook

## Purpose

This guide helps agile teams apply quality practices in a lightweight and practical way during day-to-day delivery.

## When to Use This Guide

Use this during planning, refinement, sprint execution, reviews, and release preparation.

## Core Idea

Quality in agile delivery is not a separate activity. It is built through clear expectations, early feedback, thoughtful validation, and shared ownership.

## What Teams Should Do

- clarify outcomes early
- review risks during refinement and planning
- validate work at the right level and time
- use review and release moments to confirm confidence
- capture learning from delivery and incidents

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The team understands the outcome
- [ ] Risks are discussed before build is complete
- [ ] Validation is planned and not left until the end
- [ ] The team uses review and release moments effectively
- [ ] Learning is carried forward after delivery
