# Product Owner Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps Product Owners use quality practices in a simple, practical way.

## What You Need to Know

Your role is critical in making quality real because you help define what matters, what is valuable, and what success looks like.

## What You Should Do

- clarify the desired outcome
- define clear acceptance criteria
- identify business and user risk early
- support prioritization based on impact and urgency
- help the team understand what evidence is needed before release

## Questions to Ask

- What outcome matters most?
- What happens if this is wrong?
- What are the must-have behaviors?
- What risks should we treat seriously?
- What evidence should we review before release?

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- leaving too much ambiguity in the requirement
- treating all stories as equal in risk
- expecting testing to replace clear expectations
- delaying quality discussion until late in delivery

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] Outcome is clear
- [ ] Acceptance criteria are understandable
- [ ] Risks are visible
- [ ] Validation expectations are discussed
- [ ] Release readiness is planned
