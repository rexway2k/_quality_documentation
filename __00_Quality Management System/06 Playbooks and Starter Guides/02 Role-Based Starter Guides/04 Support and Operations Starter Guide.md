# Support and Operations Starter Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps support and operations teams understand how quality work connects to production issues and service stability.

## What You Need to Know

Support and operations play a critical role in validating whether a change behaves well in real conditions and whether the team learns from incidents.

## What You Should Do

- review production behavior and service impact
- share incident findings clearly
- support release readiness and rollback discussions
- help identify recurring issues and improvement opportunities

## Questions to Ask

- What changed before the issue appeared?
- What evidence do we have about the impact?
- What monitoring or observability is available?
- What should the team learn from this issue?

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- treating support feedback as purely operational noise
- missing the connection between incidents and prevention
- delaying communication about issues and impact

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] The issue impact is understood
- [ ] The change or trigger is identified
- [ ] Monitoring evidence is collected
- [ ] The team understands the next action
- [ ] Follow-up learning is captured
